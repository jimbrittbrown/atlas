# Atlas Institute v1.0

Atlas Institute is the Atlas Organizational Learning System.

## Responsibilities
- Capture lessons learned, experiments, best practices, and failures.
- Maintain standards, playbooks, prompt libraries, architectural decisions, and business knowledge.
- Transform accumulated experience into synthesized organizational guidance.
- Recommend process improvements from cross-service operational evidence.

## Non-Responsibilities
- No workflow ownership.
- No execution ownership.
- No orchestration responsibility.
- No Executive decision ownership.
