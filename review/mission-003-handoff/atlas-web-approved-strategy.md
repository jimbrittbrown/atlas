# Atlas Web Approved Strategy

## Mission Context
- Status: pre-launch strategy approved, production not approved yet.
- Objective: create confidence that Atlas Web can improve qualified leads and booked opportunities for service businesses.
- Primary public CTA: Request Your Free Website Audit.

## Approved Strategic Decisions
1. Public-facing messaging must emphasize measurable business improvement, not website design alone.
2. Internal Atlas operating systems, internal architecture, and orchestration must remain invisible to customers.
3. Use "evidence-backed" language in public copy.
4. Homepage must show immediate visual proof through a prominent before-and-after transformation.
5. Demonstration Projects appears before detailed Process content in primary journey and homepage sequencing.
6. Demonstration work must be clearly disclosed and never presented as prior client work.
7. Website quality must itself function as first proof of capability: premium, modern, trustworthy, visually sophisticated.
8. No unsupported claims, invented proof, or guaranteed outcome statements.

## Public Positioning
- Atlas Web builds evidence-backed, conversion-focused websites for local and regional service businesses.
- The commercial promise is measurable improvement in qualified leads and booked opportunities.
- Atlas Web combines premium visual execution with practical conversion architecture and clear accountability.

## Customer-Facing Messaging Priorities
1. Business outcomes and conversion improvement.
2. Qualified leads and booked opportunities.
3. Evidence-backed design decisions.
4. Clear accountability and transparent process.
5. Premium visual quality and trust.

## Homepage Priority Framework
1. Navigation
2. Visually exceptional hero
3. Immediate transformation proof (before/after)
4. Core business outcome proposition
5. Demonstration project previews
6. Evidence-backed design principles
7. Free Website Audit offer
8. Concise process summary
9. Final CTA
10. Footer

## Language and Claims Guardrails
- Use: evidence-backed, measurable, qualified leads, booked opportunities, business outcomes, accountable.
- Avoid: AI buzzwords, technical implementation language, unsupported superlatives, guaranteed growth/revenue claims.
- Mandatory disclosure anywhere demonstration proof appears:
  - Prepared by Atlas as a demonstration of our recommended improvements.

## Stop Gate
- Do not build, publish, or contact prospects until CEO approves this handoff package and production prompts.
