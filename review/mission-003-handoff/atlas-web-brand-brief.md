# Atlas Web Brand Brief

## Brand Promise
Atlas Web creates premium, evidence-backed websites designed to improve qualified leads and booked opportunities for service businesses.

## Audience
- Primary: owner-led local and regional service businesses.
- Initial flagship segment: roofing businesses evaluating conversion improvement.

## Desired Perception
- Premium
- Modern
- Highly capable
- Trustworthy
- Commercially intelligent
- Visually sophisticated
- Calm and confident
- Outcome focused

## Brand Personality
- Clear
- Composed
- Practical
- Precise
- Confident without hype

## Voice and Tone
- Voice: expert guide with commercial clarity.
- Tone: concise, calm, accountable, and direct.
- Sentence style: plain-language outcomes first, method second.

## Messaging Hierarchy
1. We improve qualified leads and booked opportunities.
2. We do it with premium, conversion-focused website execution.
3. We use evidence-backed recommendations and clear accountability.
4. We provide a low-friction first step: Free Website Audit.

## Words to Use
- evidence-backed
- conversion-focused
- measurable improvement
- qualified leads
- booked opportunities
- accountable
- premium
- clear

## Words to Avoid
- revolutionary
- cutting-edge
- game-changing
- next-gen
- disruptive AI
- autonomous architecture
- orchestration engine

## Ethical Claims Policy
- Every factual claim must be verifiable.
- Do not imply client outcomes that do not exist.
- Do not publish fabricated social proof.
- Do not claim guaranteed business results.

## Demonstration Disclosure Policy
- Any demonstration project must be clearly marked with:
  - Prepared by Atlas as a demonstration of our recommended improvements.
- Disclosure must be visible near project title or proof visual, not hidden in fine print.
- Demonstrations may show capability but must never be framed as client engagements.
