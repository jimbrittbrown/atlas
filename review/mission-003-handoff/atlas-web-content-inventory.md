# Atlas Web Content Inventory

## Inventory Legend
- Existing: available now from approved strategy and mission facts.
- Draftable Now: can be drafted responsibly without inventing evidence.
- CEO Decision Required: blocked pending explicit approval or missing fact.

## Existing Content
- Core positioning: business outcomes, qualified leads, booked opportunities.
- Primary CTA: Request Your Free Website Audit.
- Demonstration disclosure statement.
- Initial target segment and flagship offer framing.

## Draftable Now Content
- Hero copy options centered on measurable improvement.
- Core value proposition statements.
- Free audit offer explanation.
- Process summary language.
- Trust language based on accountability and transparency.
- Contact page lead-in and response expectation placeholders.

## CEO Decision Required Content
- Legal business contact details for footer and contact page.
- Privacy policy final legal text.
- Final response SLA commitment.
- Final qualification criteria wording for audit form.

## Content Risk Controls
- Any metric-like statement must be tagged "demonstration" unless proven by real customer data.
- Remove internal architecture language from all public copy drafts.
- Reject placeholders before production approval.
