# Midnight Archives Content Strategy

## Episode Format
Cinematic micro-horror episode with one central conflict, one escalation chain, and one definitive final reveal.

## Target Length
- Target: 45-60 seconds.
- Acceptable range: 35-70 seconds when story integrity requires it.

## Opening Hook
- Must establish risk, anomaly, or emotional disturbance immediately.
- Hook objective: create urgency and curiosity before scene exposition.

## Story Structure
1. Hook: immediate tension signal.
2. Setup: world and stakes.
3. Escalation: progressive threat revelation.
4. Turn: twist or truth exposure.
5. Aftershock: final emotional imprint.

## Call to Action
- Keep CTA short and thematic.
- Prioritize episode continuity and return viewing behavior.
- Example intent: invite viewers to decode recurring clues.

## Metadata Philosophy
- Metadata must describe experience, not spoil narrative payoff.
- Titles should pair mystery with emotional promise.
- Descriptions should reinforce tone and universe continuity.

## SEO Philosophy
- Use genre-intent language aligned to horror discovery behavior.
- Balance evergreen discoverability with episode-specific specificity.
- Optimize for relevance and retention quality, not keyword stuffing.

## Playlist Strategy
1. Core arc playlists by story continuity.
2. Theme playlists by horror substyle.
3. Character-centric playlists for repeated audience engagement.
4. New viewer onboarding playlist for first-session retention.
