# Work Order 012 - Business Factory v1.0 Responsibility Matrix

Date: 2026-07-05
Status: PLANNING ONLY

| Layer / Service | Responsibility | Out of Scope for Business Factory |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Executive Service | Strategic direction and objective ownership | Delegating strategic intent outside Executive layer |
| Business Factory (Proposed) | Business blueprint creation, template standardization, playbook synthesis, and improvement guidance | Runtime orchestration, approval authority, worker coordination |
| Atlas Institute | Organizational learning and synthesized institutional knowledge | Business factory execution ownership |
| Approval Service | Authorization and policy gate decisions | Blueprint synthesis ownership |
| Worker Orchestration | Worker discovery/selection/dispatch/coordination | Business blueprint policy ownership |
| Capability Registry | Capability metadata and discovery authority | Blueprint orchestration and execution control |
| Control Center | Observational operational visibility | Blueprint lifecycle ownership |

## Boundary Rules
- Business Factory composes existing authoritative outputs without changing service ownership boundaries.
- Business Factory guidance must remain traceable, versioned, and governance-compatible.
- Implementation work remains blocked until explicit CEO approval is recorded.
