# Work Order 012 Planning - Business Factory v1.0

Date: 2026-07-05
Status: PLANNING ONLY
Implementation Authorization: NOT APPROVED

## Objective
Define Business Factory v1.0 as the Atlas capability layer for repeatable business system creation, lifecycle templates, and institutionalized operational rollout standards.

## Planning Scope
- Define standardized factory blueprints for business capability creation.
- Define reusable templates for launch, validation, scaling, and governance checkpoints.
- Define interfaces for business playbook retrieval and factory lifecycle visibility.
- Define integration boundaries with Executive, Research, Memory, Metrics, Performance Intelligence, Approval, Capability Registry, Worker Orchestration, Control Center, and Atlas Institute.

## Non-Goals
- No implementation, runtime scaffolding, or execution orchestration.
- No redesign of existing architecture or ownership boundaries.
- No transfer of strategic decision ownership from Executive layer.

## Dependencies
- Executive Service v1.0
- Research Service v1.0
- Memory Service v1.0
- Metrics Service v1.0
- Performance Intelligence Service v1.0
- Approval Service v1.0
- Capability Registry Service v1.0
- Worker Orchestration Service v1.0
- Control Center v1.0
- Atlas Institute v1.0

## Planned Deliverables
- Business Factory public interface proposal.
- Responsibility matrix and ownership boundaries.
- Risk assessment and mitigation plan.
- Readiness checklist for implementation approval gate.

## Exit Criteria
- Planning artifacts are complete and architecture consistent.
- Scope and non-goals are explicit and governance-aligned.
- Work Order 012 remains blocked from implementation until CEO approval is explicitly granted.
