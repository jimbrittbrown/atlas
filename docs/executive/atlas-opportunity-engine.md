# Atlas Opportunity Engine

Status: Executive Capability Foundation

Purpose:
The Opportunity Engine continuously discovers, evaluates, ranks, and recommends high-probability digital business opportunities aligned to CEO objectives.

## Core Mission
Atlas does not select ideas randomly.
Atlas performs executive investment decision support.

## Input
- CEO Objective
- Discovery Context (candidate opportunities)

## Pipeline
1. Market Discovery
2. Opportunity Research
3. Competitive Analysis
4. Business Model Analysis
5. Financial Estimate
6. Risk Assessment
7. Strategic Fit
8. Ranked Recommendations
9. CEO Decision

## Opportunity Scorecard Dimensions
- Probability of Success
- Time to First Revenue
- Startup Cost
- Automation Potential
- Scalability
- Competition
- Required AI Specialists
- Atlas Strategic Value
- Long-Term Potential
- Overall Executive Score

## Output Contract
Top 10 recommendations include:
- Business description
- Why Atlas recommends it
- Required specialists
- Estimated startup cost
- Estimated time to launch
- Estimated time to first revenue
- Risks
- Confidence
- Executive recommendation

## Permanent Portfolios
Portfolio A: Cash Flow
- Mission: Generate revenue quickly to fund Atlas.

Portfolio B: Enterprise
- Mission: Build long-term strategic companies.

## CEO Dashboard Model Fields
- Recommended Business
- Current Rank
- Expected ROI
- Estimated Time to Revenue
- Required Specialists
- Launch Recommendation
