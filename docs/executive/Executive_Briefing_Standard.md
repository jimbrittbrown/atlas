# Executive Briefing Standard

## Purpose

The Executive Briefing is the primary interface between the CEO and Atlas.

Every Executive Request shall produce a briefing before execution.

---

## Required Sections

1. CEO Objective

2. Executive Decision

3. Executive Assessment

4. Current Situation

5. Strategic Options

6. Recommended Strategy

7. Required Departments

8. Required Workers

9. Risks

10. Missing Information

11. Executive Recommendation

12. Next Executive Action

---

## Design Principle

The Executive Briefing should answer every question a CEO would naturally ask before approving execution.

The CEO should be able to make an informed decision without reading technical implementation details.
