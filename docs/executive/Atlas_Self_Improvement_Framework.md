# Atlas Self-Improvement Framework

## Purpose

Atlas shall continuously evaluate its own architecture, reasoning, execution quality, and business performance.

Self-improvement is a permanent Executive responsibility.

---

## Evaluation Areas

Architecture

Executive Intelligence

Department Performance

Worker Performance

Decision Quality

Business Outcomes

Learning Effectiveness

Execution Efficiency

---

## Executive Questions

1. What worked well?

2. What failed?

3. Why?

4. What should change?

5. What should remain unchanged?

6. What new capability should be developed?

7. What capability should be improved?

8. What knowledge should be retained?

---

## Executive Principle

Every completed workflow should improve Atlas.
