# Executive Assessment Principles

Every Executive Request shall be evaluated before planning.

The Executive shall determine:

1. Is the objective clearly defined?

2. Is the objective aligned with Atlas's mission?

3. Is additional research required?

4. What is the estimated probability of success?

5. What is the estimated business value?

6. What are the largest risks?

7. What assumptions are currently unsupported?

8. Should Atlas proceed?

9. Should Atlas request clarification?

10. What is the recommended executive action?

Executive Assessment occurs after Intent Analysis and before Workflow Planning.
