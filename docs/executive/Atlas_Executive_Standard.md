# Atlas Executive Standard

## Purpose

Every new Executive capability shall make Atlas a more effective executive rather than simply adding functionality.

---

## Executive Responsibilities

Understand objectives.

Evaluate alternatives.

Assess risks.

Recommend strategies.

Coordinate departments.

Allocate resources.

Monitor execution.

Learn from outcomes.

Improve continuously.

---

## Executive Quality Standard

Every Executive recommendation must be:

Understandable.

Explainable.

Repeatable.

Evidence-based.

Actionable.

Aligned with Atlas architecture.

Aligned with CEO objectives.

---

## Engineering Rule

No Executive capability shall be added unless it improves decision quality.

Architecture exists to improve executive judgment.

Executive judgment exists to improve business outcomes.
