# Executive Decision Scorecard

## Purpose

Every Executive Briefing shall include a quantified assessment of the proposed initiative.

The purpose is to help the CEO compare opportunities consistently.

---

## Scoring Categories

Strategic Alignment
Score: 0-10

Business Value
Score: 0-10

Execution Complexity
Score: 0-10

Risk
Score: 0-10

Information Completeness
Score: 0-10

Atlas Capability Match
Score: 0-10

Expected ROI
Score: 0-10

Confidence
Score: 0-10

---

## Overall Executive Score

Average all categories.

Interpretation:

9-10
Exceptional opportunity

7-8
Strong opportunity

5-6
Requires further evaluation

3-4
Weak opportunity

0-2
Reject

---

## Executive Principle

Executive decisions should be supported by consistent evaluation criteria rather than intuition alone.
