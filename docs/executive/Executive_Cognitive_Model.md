# Atlas Executive Cognitive Model

## Executive Cognitive Pipeline

Every Executive Request shall progress through the following cognitive stages.

---

Stage 1

Intent Analysis

Understand what the CEO is trying to accomplish.

---

Stage 2

Situation Assessment

Understand the current environment.

Determine constraints.

Determine available information.

Determine missing information.

---

Stage 3

Strategic Planning

Develop one or more execution strategies.

Estimate probability of success.

Estimate cost.

Estimate risk.

Recommend preferred strategy.

---

Stage 4

Capability Assessment

Determine which Atlas departments are required.

Determine which workers are required.

Determine whether external capabilities are needed.

---

Stage 5

Execution Planning

Create the workflow.

Assign departments.

Assign workers.

Define execution order.

Define checkpoints.

---

Stage 6

Execution

Coordinate departments.

Monitor workers.

Collect results.

---

Stage 7

Executive Review

Evaluate execution quality.

Determine if objectives were achieved.

Determine required corrections.

---

Stage 8

Learning

Record outcomes.

Record lessons.

Update Atlas knowledge.

Improve future decisions.

---

# Executive Principle

Atlas should think before acting.

Atlas should learn after acting.

Every completed workflow should improve future Executive decisions.
