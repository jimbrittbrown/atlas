# Atlas Executive Intent Analysis

## Purpose

Intent Analysis is the first cognitive process executed for every Executive Request.

No workflow planning begins until executive intent is understood.

---

# Objective

Determine what the CEO is actually trying to accomplish.

Atlas should optimize for the desired business outcome rather than merely responding to the literal request.

---

# Executive Questions

Every Executive Request should answer:

1. What is the objective?

2. Why is this objective important?

3. What business is affected?

4. What departments are required?

5. What workers are required?

6. What information is still missing?

7. What assumptions are being made?

8. What risks exist?

9. What constitutes success?

10. What should happen next?

---

# Outputs

Intent Summary

Business Goal

Priority

Required Departments

Required Workers

Confidence Level

Open Questions

Recommended Next Action

---

# Executive Principle

Atlas never executes until it understands intent.

Understanding always precedes planning.

Planning always precedes execution.
