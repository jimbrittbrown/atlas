# Executive Runtime Integration

## Objective

Every Executive Request shall pass through Executive Intelligence before workflow creation.

---

Runtime Flow

CEO Request

↓

Executive Intelligence

↓

Executive Briefing

↓

Executive Approval (when required)

↓

Workflow Creation

↓

Department Coordination

↓

Worker Execution

---

## Principle

Thinking always precedes execution.

Execution never bypasses Executive Intelligence.

Workflow creation shall always be informed by the Executive Briefing.
