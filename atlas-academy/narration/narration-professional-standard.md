# Narration Director Professional Standard v1

## Purpose

Define professional documentary narration standards so voice output improves clarity, emotional credibility, and audience retention.

## Definition Of Professional Documentary Narration

Professional narration must:
- Sound natural and human-paced.
- Use strategic pauses to improve comprehension and emphasis.
- Match emotional tone to documentary stakes.
- Maintain tone consistency across the full narrative.
- Keep listeners engaged without sounding theatrical or robotic.
- Preserve clear articulation and intelligibility.
- Handle pronunciation of names, places, and technical terms reliably.

## Measurable Evaluation Criteria

### 1. Natural Flow
- Criterion: Delivery sounds conversational and coherent, not mechanical.
- Metric: Natural Flow score (0-10).
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

### 2. Emotional Delivery
- Criterion: Vocal emphasis matches narrative stakes and intent.
- Metric: Emotional Delivery score (0-10).
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

### 3. Pacing
- Criterion: Speaking speed and pauses support understanding and tension control.
- Metric: Pacing score (0-10).
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

### 4. Clarity
- Criterion: Listener can understand words and sentence structure without strain.
- Metric: Clarity score (0-10).
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

### 5. Listener Engagement
- Criterion: Delivery sustains attention through progression and vocal variation.
- Metric: Listener Engagement score (0-10).
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

### 6. Overall Narration Quality
- Criterion: Composite quality across all narration dimensions.
- Metric: Overall Narration Quality score (0-10).
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

## Quality Gate Rules

- Fail if any category score < 5.
- Conditional pass if overall score is between 6.0 and 6.9 with no category < 5.
- Professional pass if overall score >= 7.0 and no category < 5.

## Required Evaluation Output

- Category scores
- Overall score
- Diagnosis summary
- Recommended improvements
- Classification: PASS / CONDITIONAL / FAIL
