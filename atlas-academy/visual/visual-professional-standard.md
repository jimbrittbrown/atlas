# Visual Director Professional Standard v1

## Purpose

Define professional-quality documentary visual standards so generated visuals strengthen narrative credibility, emotional impact, and viewer retention.

## Definition Of Professional Documentary Visuals

Professional documentary visuals must:
- Directly support the story being told.
- Preserve historical and contextual accuracy.
- Reinforce intended emotional tone without distortion.
- Maintain continuity across scenes.
- Use clear cinematic composition to guide viewer attention.
- Avoid stylistic choices that weaken trust or clarity.

## Measurable Evaluation Criteria

### 1. Story Relevance
- Criterion: Each visual clearly serves the narrative beat.
- Metric: Story Relevance score (0-10).
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

### 2. Historical Accuracy
- Criterion: Visual details align with documented context and period realism.
- Metric: Historical Accuracy score (0-10).
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

### 3. Emotional Impact
- Criterion: Visual tone supports intended audience feeling and stakes.
- Metric: Emotional Impact score (0-10).
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

### 4. Visual Continuity
- Criterion: Scene-to-scene consistency is maintained in style, character, and setting cues.
- Metric: Visual Continuity score (0-10).
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

### 5. Cinematic Composition
- Criterion: Framing, depth, focus, and visual hierarchy improve clarity and engagement.
- Metric: Cinematic Composition score (0-10).
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

### 6. Scene Quality
- Criterion: Overall scene specification quality is production-ready.
- Metric: Scene Quality score (0-10).
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

### 7. Overall Visual Quality
- Criterion: Composite quality across all six dimensions.
- Metric: Overall score (0-10).
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

## Quality Gate Rules

- Fail if any category score < 5.
- Conditional pass if overall score is between 6.0 and 6.9 with no category < 5.
- Professional pass if overall score >= 7.0 and no category < 5.

## Required Evaluation Output

- Category scores
- Overall score
- Recommendations
- Classification: PASS / CONDITIONAL / FAIL
