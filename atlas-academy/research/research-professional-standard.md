# Research Worker Professional Standard v1

## Purpose

Define professional-quality documentary research standards so Storytelling receives stronger, verifiable, and production-useful research inputs.

## Definition Of Professional-Quality Documentary Research

Professional documentary research must be:
- Source-grounded and traceable.
- Evidence-weighted rather than opinion-driven.
- Story-useful for documentary production.
- Novel enough to avoid generic outputs.
- Audience-relevant for the target documentary viewer.
- Complete enough to support narrative decisions.

## Measurable Evaluation Criteria

### 1. Source Quality
- Criterion: Sources are credible, attributable, and context-appropriate.
- Metric: Source Quality score 0-10.
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

### 2. Evidence Strength
- Criterion: Claims are supported by concrete evidence and corroboration.
- Metric: Evidence Strength score 0-10.
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

### 3. Story Potential
- Criterion: Findings contain meaningful stakes, tension, or transformation.
- Metric: Story Potential score 0-10.
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

### 4. Novelty
- Criterion: Research contains non-obvious, differentiating insights.
- Metric: Novelty score 0-10.
- Professional threshold: >= 6.

### 5. Audience Interest
- Criterion: Findings are likely to sustain audience curiosity and relevance.
- Metric: Audience Interest score 0-10.
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

### 6. Completeness
- Criterion: Research package has enough breadth and depth to inform storytelling.
- Metric: Completeness score 0-10.
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

### 7. Overall Research Quality
- Criterion: Composite quality across all six dimensions.
- Metric: Overall score 0-10.
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

## Quality Gate Rules

- Fail gate if any category < 5.
- Conditional pass if overall is between 6.0 and 6.9 with no category < 5.
- Professional pass if overall >= 7.0 and all categories >= 5.

## Required Evaluation Output

- Category scores
- Overall score
- Recommendations
- Classification: PASS / CONDITIONAL / FAIL
