# Storytelling Worker Professional Standard v1

## Purpose

Define professional-quality documentary storytelling standards for the Storytelling Worker so output quality and audience retention improve measurably.

## Definition Of Professional-Quality Documentary Storytelling

Professional-quality storytelling:
- Earns continued audience attention segment by segment.
- Delivers clear narrative progression with meaningful stakes.
- Preserves factual and editorial integrity.
- Maintains coherence from opening through conclusion.
- Avoids filler, confusion, and manipulative framing.

## Measurable Evaluation Criteria

### 1. Opening Strength
- Criterion: The opening establishes relevance, stakes, and forward momentum.
- Metric: Opening score 0-10.
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

### 2. Curiosity Continuity
- Criterion: The narrative sustains unresolved but meaningful questions.
- Metric: Curiosity score 0-10.
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

### 3. Narrative Flow
- Criterion: Segment transitions are coherent and directional.
- Metric: Narrative Flow score 0-10.
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

### 4. Information Density
- Criterion: Information load is useful and digestible, not sparse or overwhelming.
- Metric: Information Density score 0-10.
- Professional threshold: >= 6.

### 5. Audience Commitment
- Criterion: The script repeatedly creates reasons to keep watching.
- Metric: Audience Commitment score 0-10.
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

### 6. Overall Storytelling Quality
- Criterion: Composite quality across all five dimensions.
- Metric: Overall score 0-10.
- Professional threshold: >= 7.

## Quality Gate Rules

- Fail gate if any of the following occur:
  - Opening Strength < 5
  - Narrative Flow < 5
  - Audience Commitment < 5
- Conditional pass if overall score is between 6.0 and 6.9 with no hard failures.
- Professional pass if overall score >= 7.0 and no hard failures.

## Required Output For Each Script Evaluation

- Category scores
- Overall score
- Improvement recommendations
- Pass/Conditional/Fail classification
